Introduction

Our Website Now do support PWA or Pogressive Web App feature. Now What is PWA? It is the lite weight chrome-based standalone viewport of the website. It works and function like a Native App.

Why PWA

It is fast, easy to use and does support offline functionality. Plus we can target specific contents to the PWA only that can may not be available in the website. And we will target Notifications to the PWA.

How to use PWA

When this page is open a pop will ask you to ACCEPT NOTIFICATION from us. ACCEPT it then Chrome will ask you to ALLOW notification. Once you Accpt them a banner will pop-up on the bottom of your screen, saying "Add to Homescreen foodyC." Click on that. Wait until it is downloaded and install. And now find it on your Applist.

Use Native App

We also have our good old APK. It is fully functional and a form of PWApk that is delivered over TWA or Trusted Web Activity with SHA-256 Encription. Just Download and use it. It will prompt to 'Allow App from Unknown Sources', Google Play Protect may ask you to send Report as it is still now not registered on PlayStote.
